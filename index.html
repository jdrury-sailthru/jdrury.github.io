Tutorial: adding a new component to an AngularJS 1.5+ app

Feb 17, 2018 The design and implementation of AngularJS applications has evolved signicantly since the framework hit 1.0 in June, 2012. In the early period, most applications were written with the ng-controller paradigm. A controller was attached to a template, and the template had access to the controller’s $scope.
